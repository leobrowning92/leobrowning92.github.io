Work

I am a 23 year old PhD candidate with The MacDiarmid Institute for Advanced Materials and Nanotechnology at Victoria University of Wellington on silver nanowire based neural networks and CNT FET's for device and sensor applications.

My work consists of microfabrication techniques and nanowire synthesis to produce electrical devices in Victoria University's Cleanroom fabrication facillity. After creation SEM, AFM and IV spectroscopy are used to characterize devices.
